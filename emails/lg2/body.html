Cyber Security

 

Dear Colleagues

Urgent reminder to complete your Cyber Security Awareness Training in MyUni

The Chief Operating Officer, Bruce Lines, emailed all staff at the University last month to highlight the importance of being safe online and protecting data.

The importance of this has been underlined by recent cyber breaches experienced by the Federal Government and higher education institutions. 

According to our records, you have not yet completed the mandatory Cyber Security Awareness Training(https://theuniversityofadelaide.cmail20.com/t/i-l-pikgo-jrdduhutty-y/) in MyUni.

One of our most vital lines of defence against cyber attack is in the care and precautions taken by staff. The security training will help you to take the necessary precautions, and be alert to possible risks.

Human errors play a significant role in triggering security incidents, so this training is more than just ‘ticking a box’. It takes just 20-30 minutes. You can also find a range of helpful resources on the website(https://theuniversityofadelaide.cmail20.com/t/i-l-pikgo-jrdduhutty-j/).

We are all responsible for data safety and security, so thank you for doing your part to help keep our University safe from the risks of data breaches and attacks.

If you have any questions, please contact the ITDS Service Desk on (08) 8313 3000 or by emailing servicedesk@adelaide.edu.au.

Yours sincerely

Bev Wright

Chief Information Officer
